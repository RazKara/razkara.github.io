Who I Am

Engineer. Communicator.
Problem Solver.

I am a versatile Electrical and Sanitation Engineer with 6+ years of operational leadership in one of Algeria's most demanding environments — the Illizi region, a remote Saharan province bordering Libya and Niger at the heart of Algeria's oil and gas fields.

My profile is rare: I hold a Master's in Renewable Energy, a Bachelor's in Control & Diagnosis of Electrical Systems, and a Bachelor's in English Language — allowing me to bridge deep technical expertise with institutional communication and international collaboration without any translation dependency.

Certified in PLC/DCS automation (Siemens & Yokogawa), Gas Turbine Control, and emergency response, I thrive in resource-constrained, high-stakes environments where most engineers never operate. I am currently open to senior operations, inspection, infrastructure management, or international advisory roles.
